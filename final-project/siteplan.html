Movie Mania - Site Plan

Site Name

Movie Mania - a platform dedicated to movie lovers like me!. The site provides information about various movies, including summaries, genres, release years, trailers, and ratings. Users can browse through a list of films, filter by categories such as action, comedy, drama, animation and more, and even mark their favorites. The website will be designed to be visually engaging and responsive across different devices.

Site Purpose

The site is a user-friendly hub for movie lovers to discover, filter, and shortlist films. It provides:

  • Browseable catalog with summaries, genres, release year, and ratings.
  • Search and filters (e.g., genre, decade, runtime).
  • Trailer links and detail pages with key facts.
  • “Favorites” saved in the browser (localStorage) to revisit later.
  • Lightweight, responsive design for mobile and desktop.

Scenarios (Target-Audience Questions)

  • “What are the top-rated action movies under 2 hours that I can watch this weekend?”
  • “I love period dramas—can I filter by genre and decade to find new titles?”
  • “Where can I see trailers and save my favorites to check later?”
  • “Which movies similar to my favorites are trending this month?”
